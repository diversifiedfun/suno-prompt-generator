# Burning Man — Story Set (deep house → melodic techno journey)

Built in Suno Prompt Studio **Story mode**. One narrative → 8 tracks that trace the day.

**Story:** Dawn on the playa, dust still pink, I'm on my bike in a fur coat and goggles gliding past the temple as the heat wakes up. By afternoon it's glitter and sweat, sun hammering, everyone dancing on an art car. Deep night the crowd swallows me, bass in my chest, lights everywhere. Then sunrise — dust settling, strangers hugging, the whole city glowing and spent.

**Genres:** Deep house, Melodic techno  ·  **Arc:** sustained-peak  ·  **Contour:** 6 7 7 8 8 9 9 8
