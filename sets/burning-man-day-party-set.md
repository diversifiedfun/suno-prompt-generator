# 2-Hour Burning Man Day-Party Set — "UNDER THE MISTERS"

Distrikt / Pink Mammoth **hot-afternoon peak**. High noon, misters on, sweat, sun
overhead, bodies moving, energy pinned the whole way. **NOT** a sunrise→sunset journey —
this is sustained peak heat. No ambient intros, no comedown, no weird/psychedelic
(that's 3am). Every track: feel-it-in-your-body groove, sexy female vocals, ~123–126 BPM.

  long DJ outro" (mixable length, but the intro DRIVES — no ambient pads).
- Expanded lyrics, afternoon/heat/misters/sweat/body theme (never sunrise or sunset).
- Vocal Gender: **Female**. **Weirdness stays 50%** (default = on-target; never raise it).
- ~18 tracks @ ~6 min ≈ 2 hrs. Energy varies by TEXTURE (funky → disco → dirty → afro →
  vocal peak), never by dropping the energy.
- **Slot: 2–4pm, PEAK.** You're the headliner — even song 1 lands on a packed, hands-up
  floor. No gentle warm-up, no sunset comedown. Energy floor is HIGH the whole set.
- **Bass escalates as we go:** warm/punchy funky bass on the openers → rounder/deeper mid
  → filthy, heavy-hitting distorted bass at the nasty peak (songs 10–13).

## DIALED RECIPE (locked after live testing)

  its own VOCAL FORM (sung diva / group chant / sleazy spoken / call-and-response).

  relentless, hands-up, euphoric, massive." Texture can change; energy can't drop.
